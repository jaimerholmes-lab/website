✿ About χatχat Juice Shop ✿

χatχat Juice Shop creates handcrafted juices inspired by fresh fruit, traditional plant knowledge, and community. Made by two girlies who love taking care of our community through and healing through harvest. Seasonal plant blends gathered in relation to coastal and northern British Columbia ecosystems, reflecting body cycles and land cycles
